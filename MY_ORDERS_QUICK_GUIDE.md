# 📋 My Orders - Quick Guide

## 🎯 What Changed?

### ✅ NEW: Welcome Alert Banner
When you have orders, you'll see a green banner at the top that says:

```
✓ شكراً لك على طلبك! ✨
  تم استقبال طلبك بنجاح
```

This tells you that your order was received and reminds you that you can cancel it within **6 hours**.

---

### ✅ NEW: Cancel Order Button
Instead of viewing full details, you now see a **"Cancel Order"** button directly in your order.

**When can you cancel?**
- ✅ Order must be **pending** (just received)
- ✅ Order must be within **6 hours** of creation
- ❌ After 6 hours: Button disappears (can't cancel)
- ❌ If status is not pending: Button doesn't show

---

## 📋 How to Cancel an Order

### Step 1: Go to "My Orders"
- Click on your account
- Select "طلباتي" (My Orders)

### Step 2: Find Your Order
- Look for the order you want to cancel
- Make sure it shows **pending** status
- Make sure you created it less than 6 hours ago

### Step 3: Expand Order (if needed)
- Click the **+** icon to expand order details
- Or it might be expanded already

### Step 4: Click "Cancel Order" Button
- You'll see the red button with trash icon
- Click it to open the cancellation modal

### Step 5: Confirm Cancellation
A popup will appear asking:
```
هل أنت متأكد من رغبتك في إلغاء الطلب ABC123؟
```

You can:
- **Optional:** Add a reason (اختياري)
- **Click:** تأكيد الإلغاء (Confirm Cancel)
- **Or:** الرجوع (Go Back)

### Step 6: See Confirmation
- You'll get a green notification: "تم الإلغاء بنجاح" ✓
- The order status changes to **cancelled**
- The cancel button disappears

---

## 💰 Refund Information

When you cancel an order:
- ✅ Your money will be refunded
- ⏱️ Takes 3-5 business days
- 📱 You'll be notified

## ❓ FAQs

### Q: Why can't I cancel my order?

**Answer:** Check:
1. Is status **pending**? (Can't cancel if approved, preparing, shipped, etc.)
2. Is it within **6 hours**? (You have 6 hours from creation)
3. Is the button showing? (If not, you're not eligible)

### Q: What if 6 hours have passed?

**Answer:** You can't cancel automatically, but:
- Contact customer service
- Call: +964 XXX XXX XXXX
- They might help manually cancel

### Q: Can I see my cancellation reason later?

**Answer:** Yes, the reason you provide is saved. When you view your cancelled order, you can see:
- Why you cancelled
- When you cancelled
- Order history

### Q: How do I track my refund?

**Answer:** After cancellation:
1. Go to Track Order
2. Enter your order code + phone
3. Status shows: **cancelled**
4. Wait 3-5 business days for refund
